Important Announcements

Third Attempt Candidates

Consequent to the recent order of the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India (pertaining to Writ Petition (Civil) Nos. 854/2024 and 17/2025) dated 10.01.2025, students who appeared in their class 12 examination for the first time in 2023 AND have withdrawn from their courses and dropped out between 05.11.2024 to 18.11.2024 would be permitted to register for JEE (Advanced) 2025.
